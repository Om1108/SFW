Introduction

Sheela C Shetty, a trailblazer in Kannada fashion journalism, has played a pivotal role in bridging the gap between fashion and the Kannada-speaking audience. As the first journalist in her field, she has paved the way for future generations of fashion writers in the region. Her extensive career has seen her meet and interview prominent Bollywood and Sandalwood actors, offering unique insights into their style and fashion choices. Beyond celebrity interactions, Sheela has covered numerous high-profile fashion events, showcasing trends and talents within the industry. Her contributions have been recognized through multiple prestigious awards, celebrating her as a key figure in shaping Kannada fashion media. As a member of the Karnataka Press Club, she continues to advocate for fashion journalism in regional languages, ensuring that the voices of local designers and trends reach a broader audience. Her influence has earned her a distinguished place in the Kannada fashion industry, where she remains a respected and inspiring figure for both her peers and aspiring journalists.
